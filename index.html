Introduction

I'm a student at Southern University of Science and Technology (SUSTech) in Shenzhen, China. And I'm a member of Fields Honor class which is an elite program for mathematic undergraduates.

My research interest is in optimization, numerical methods and other related fields. Before I got involved in this area, I was studying finite group theory, it's quite hard but interesting. And I had a good time during that period. But then in the end of my sophomore year, I found that applied mathematics is more attractive for me. Besides enjoying the happiness brought by proving step by step, I can see how it affects the real world as well. And that's the reason why I am here.
